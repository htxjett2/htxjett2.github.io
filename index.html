About LACRP

Los Angeles County Roleplay (LACRP) is a community-focused FiveM server emphasizing immersive, realistic roleplay across law enforcement, EMS, civilian life and more. We're friendly to new players and supportive of experienced roleplayers.

How to Join

  1. Join our Discord: discord.gg/lacountyrp
  2. Follow the rules and read #welcome for instructions.
  3. Download recommended FiveM resources from the pinned Discord messages.
  4. Connect to the server IP (posted in Discord) and enjoy roleplay.
